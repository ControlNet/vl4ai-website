PhD Positions

We are always looking for a highly motivated and outstanding candidate, who is eager to get involved in cutting edge, creative research with real-world applications.

Essential Skills:

  • You hold an Honour/Master degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics, or Engineering with a great GPA from a reputable University.
  • You have a background in machine learning and computer vision.
  • You have excellent skills in areas such as applied mathematics, statistics/probability theory or computational maths/physics.
  • You have programming skills in one or few coding languages (e.g., Python, Matlab, C/C++/C#, Java, CUDA).
  • We expect fluent communication skills in English.

Desirable Skills:
  • You are already a proficient programmer in one of the main Deep Learning libraries (e.g., Darknet, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Keras etc.).
  • Track record of a publication in top tier Computer Vision, Robotics or Artificial Intelligence conferences and/or journals would be also advantageous
