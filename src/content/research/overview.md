This section captures the lab's active research projects together with its public datasets and benchmarks.

The legacy site grouped the program around three persistent themes:

- **Perception** for object understanding, tracking, activity understanding, reconstruction, and robot perception.
- **Forecasting** for human trajectory and body-motion prediction.
- **Navigation** for active visual navigation and UAV planning under uncertainty.
