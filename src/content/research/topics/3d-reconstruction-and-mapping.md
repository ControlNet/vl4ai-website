This topic covers 3D localisation, reconstruction, and mapping of humans and objects in dynamic environments. The legacy research page framed this work as a route toward higher-level 3D scene understanding for autonomous driving, robotic interaction, and navigation.

Representative legacy publication links preserved from the old research page:

- [Normal-GS](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2410.20593)
- [TFS-NeRF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2409.17459)
- [Physically Plausible 3D Human-Scene Reconstruction from Monocular RGB Image using an Adversarial Learning Approach](http://arxiv.org/abs/2307.14570)
- [ActiveRMAP](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2211.12656.pdf)
- [ODAM](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2108.10165.pdf)
- [MO-LTR](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2012.05360.pdf)
