The legacy site described this stream as human behaviour understanding for autonomous driving, robot navigation, and surveillance: recognising individual actions, grouping people by social interaction, and inferring group-level activity. The long-term aim is a holistic, multi-task approach where group discovery, action prediction, and social activity modelling benefit one another instead of being solved independently.

Representative legacy publication links preserved from the old research page:

- [Social-MAE](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2404.05578)
- [Real-time Trajectory-based Social Group Detection](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2304.05678.pdf)
- [MARLIN](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2211.06627.pdf)
- [JRDB-Act](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2106.08827.pdf)
- [Joint learning of Social Groups, Individuals Action and Sub-group Activities in Videos](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2007.02632.pdf)
