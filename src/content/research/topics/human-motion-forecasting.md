Forecasting human trajectory and body motion is positioned on the legacy site as a core capability for robotics, healthcare, and surveillance. The emphasis is on physically and socially plausible prediction under the multimodal uncertainty that appears in real-world interaction.

Representative legacy publication links preserved from the old research page:

- [SoMoFormer](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2208.14023.pdf)
