The goal of this line of work is to enable robotic navigation where goals can be expressed at a much higher level of abstraction than traditional pipelines allow. The legacy page emphasised scene-layout prediction, active observation, and navigation that can operate in previously unseen environments.

Representative legacy publication links preserved from the old research page:

- [Hi-SLAM / Hier-SLAM](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2409.12518)
- [ActiveRMAP](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2211.12656.pdf)
- [Predicting Topological Maps for Visual Navigation in Unexplored Environments](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2211.12649.pdf)
