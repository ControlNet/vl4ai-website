This stream addresses autonomous UAV missions for localisation and tracking under noisy measurements, limited field-of-view sensing, uncertain object counts, and multi-agent coordination complexity. The legacy page framed the research as both a theoretical and applied planning problem for online search-and-track missions.

Representative legacy publication links preserved from the old research page:
